TFT: Runeterra Reforged - Teamfight Tactics

Teamfight Tactics (TFT) is an auto battler game mode where players strategically assemble a team of champions, position them on a battlefield, and watch them fight automatically against other players' teams. The goal is to build a strong team, utilize synergies, manage resources, and make strategic decisions to outlast opponents and emerge victorious.

Legends of Runeterra - Digital collectible card game

Legends of Runeterra is a digital card game set in the world of League of Legends. Players construct decks using a wide variety of champions and spells, then battle against each other. The game combines strategy, deck-building, and tactical gameplay to outwit opponents and claim victory.

Ruined King - A League of Legends Story

Ruined King is a role-playing video game set in the fantasy world of Runeterra, based on the popular League of Legends universe. Developed by Airship Syndicate and published by Riot Forge, Ruined King combines immersive storytelling, strategic turn-based combat, and exploration.
